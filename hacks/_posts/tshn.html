--- layout: hack title: TSHN ~ Top Stories of Hacker News in last 24 hours shortdescription: TSHN i.e Top Stories of Hacker News is a tool which scrapes the front page of Hacker News every 15 min. It combines all the top stories from the last 24 hours and displays it sorted via karma-points the stories have earned. zip_url: https://github.com/sushant-hiray/tshn/zipball/master issue_url: https://github.com/sushant-hiray/tshn/issues/new repository_url: http://github.com/sushant-hiray/tshn category:hacks --- Instructions ------------ Checkout `run.sh` for a sample bash script Change the path to `scrape.py` Now add a cron task as follows: 1. Open terminal and type `crontab -e` 2. Append the following line into the opened file: `*/15 * * * * ` 3. Once the cron task is set right, the script will scrape front page of HN every 15 minutes and update the top stories data accordingly. Demo ---- You can look over to www.wncc-iitb.org/tshn/ for the latest top stories.
